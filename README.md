# MAJK-Coal-Corporation
An environmentally driven enterprise focused on producing sustainable, high-efficiency briquettes made from organic and biodegradable waste materials such as coconut shells, rice husks, sawdust, and agricultural residues.

## About the Venture
MAJK Coal Corporation converts organic waste into clean, affordable, and high-efficiency eco-briquettes for daily household and small business use.

---

## Problem We Address
Many households and small food businesses spend too much on charcoal and gas. Traditional fuels are expensive, create harmful smoke, and damage the environment.

---

## Our Solution
We turn organic and biodegradable waste into eco-friendly briquettes that burn longer, produce less smoke, and cost less than traditional charcoal.

---

## Unique Value Proposition (UVP)
Affordable, high-efficiency briquettes made from recycled organic waste that help customers save money, cook cleaner, and support environmental sustainability.

---
